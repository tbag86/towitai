## Slide order
> Map each beat to a component pattern from `CLAUDE.md §4`. The shipped `deck.html` has one slide per
> pattern as a starting library — reorder/duplicate/delete to fit this narrative.

1. **Cover** — hero (image or Veo video) + proposition + lockup
2. **The proposition** — split: what it is + the ask
3. **The case** — stat grid: the numbers that make the argument
4. **The detail** — before/after or feature photos
5. **How it works** — steps
6. **Why it matters** — tick-list benefits
7. **The team** — partner cards (centrepiece)
8. **Who does what** — roles table
9. **Delivery model** — phased numbered cards
10. **The ask** — call-to-action list + headline figure
11. **Value** — quantified return (stat cards)
12. **Why this works** — without/with contrast
13. **Vision / legacy** — full-bleed cinematic moment
14. **Alignment** — mapped to the audience's priorities
15. **Risk & objections** — Q&A grid
16. **Next steps** — actions + contact
17. **Sources & assumptions** — transparency
